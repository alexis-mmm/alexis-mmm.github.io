About

Hi! I'm Xiwen Min, an M.S. student in Computer Science at New York University's Courant Institute. My research interests lie in computer systems, with a focus on scalable and efficient infrastructure for ML workloads, including 3D Gaussian Splatting training and LLM serving. At NYU, I was very fortunate to work with Prof. Jinyang Li and Prof. Aurojit Panda. I'm currently a visiting student at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Beidi Chen.

Before NYU, I received my B.Eng. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
